GENERAL GUIDELINES

Get connected and begin your career journey to any of your desired country for Job opportunities, Universities available and ready to give admission to undergraduates, Graduate Students (B.Sc. and Master’s degree Holders) who wish to further their studies abroad. We have the links that can even sponsor your education either on loan basis or scholarship if you qualify for a fully funded scholarship.
Please note that your ability to gain admission into any of the universities abroad would depend on the fact that you meet the basic admission requirements.
For instance, you must have a B.Sc. degree to be qualified for a master degree program. Your GPA must not be less than that required by the school you are applying to. So it is our job to go through your educational background to know which school you are eligible to apply and we are just here to see to it that you are given admission provided you are qualified.

Similarly, to be eligible to get your education sponsored abroad, you must be admitted into any of the universities either in United States of America or Canada with admission letter as proof. We hereby request that you provide us with the necessary documents to enable us start processing your application immediately. Your admission letter will be successfully given to you if the school deem it fit to offer you admission, at least 8 weeks (two months from the date your application is completed and your documents uploaded on the school admission portal.
Please note that we are not the school but we work with the schools following their calendar winter, summer and fall terms. Your resumption date is that determined by the school. And how soon you relocate depends on how you meet the school deadlines during your application process, that’s why we advise people to pick up our form, fill and submit on time so that we can start the process early.

STEP 1
We will review requirements and prepare your application. Please ensure you've met all the admission requirements for your program, so that your application process will go smoothly, if you submit all documents requested in time. We will go through them and match them with the admission requirements such as minimum qualifications, Minimum GPA requirement, experiences and skills you need in order to be considered for admission into a program. Each program has its own unique set of requirements, and they may differ depending on whether you've attended university before. all applications are directly handled by the consultant. Step-by-step instructions are observed, professionally. Before we begin, it is important you make sure you're ready to provide the following:
Your: First Name, Middle Name, and Sur Na which must be same with the one on your certificates and international passports.
Your Permanent Home address,
Date of birth, Email Address, Phone contact, Details of your current and previous education, including start and end dates.
Depending on your background and program of study, you may also need to provide:
Transcripts and course information from previous studies
Proof of English language proficiency which we can work out waiver
A personal statement about your goals for the program
A portfolio of your previous work
An audition or interview
Reference contact information.
These application requirements vary by program, so please be sure you pick the right form as peculiar to your program so that we can have the right information to process your application. Application and documentation deadlines vary by program. The consultation and application fee must be paid before we commence your application. All documents must also be submitted to us before the admission deadlines. Please note that admission process for international students takes time due to verification exercises the school has to carryout.

Once you've checked requirements and gathered what we need, we expect payment for your application/consultation

Education history
A complete listing of your educational background from Grade 1- 12 (or highest grade level), College and University is required as part of your application. Also required is the listing of institutions attended on an exchange or letter of permission.

Avoid application fraud or misconduct
If you commit application fraud or misconduct, you acceptance and registration may be withdrawn and you may be disqualified from consideration, not only in the year of application, but also in all subsequent sessions. Application fraud or misconduct includes, but is not limited to, the following:
Failure to declare attendance at another post-secondary institution Presenting falsified academic documentation or causing or encouraging another person to falsify records through translation or data changes Presenting falsified personal documentation, e.g. using a false name, date of birth, country of origin, etc. Presenting falsified or fictitious reference documentation Cheating on or having another person write a standardized entry exam such as TOEFL, IELTS, MCAT, LSAT or DAT Presenting another person's standardized test score as one's own to falsify a test result Failure to report suspensions from another post-secondary institution Paying for an application fee with a fraudulent credit card Making changes to your application after submission.

STEP 3
Consultation/application fee
Client must pay the Consultation/application fee of three million five hundred and thirty thousand naira. (N3, 530,000.) This amount could be paid by two installments. Firstly 530,000 is expected to be paid for mobilization which also serves as part of the client’s commitment fee. The consultant expects completion of three million naira ( N3,000,000) from the client a month’s or two after the client arrives and money is handed over to him/her for resumption of study in the country agreed and stated in the agreement. The consultation/application fee is of 530,000 is non-refundable.
The client is responsible for ensuring that all required document is submitted to the consultant for further documentation and be submitted in time to meet application deadline. The consultation form will be legally sealed in court for formal documentation. The application for admission by the consultant on behalf of the clients to the university will commence only after the consultant has received payment of five hundred and thirty thousand naira (530,000) from the client for mobilization, which forms part of school’s application fee of one hundred and fifty thousand naira (N150,000) once admission through, the client is expected to balance (N3,000,000).
The client is expected to pay five hundred and thirty thousand naira before the consultation form is processed.
The deposited amount from client could only be refunded on a condition that, the consultant is incompetent to carrying out his obligation as stated in the agreement,

How to Make Payment
The client can pay Consultation/application fee through transfer from his/her bank account to the company’s account or Wealth Akpabio’s account directly and have his/her transaction’s copy of payment for future reference.

DOCUMENT RETURN
All documents submitted in support of an application become the property of Valexerburance Consult and cannot be returned. If applicants require irreplaceable originals to be returned. Please note, after two years from the submission date, all documents will be destroyed.
The client is expected to provide two civil servant guarantors with postdated cheque signed and attached to document presented to the consultant before migrating to his/her desired country of Studies. The consultant provides a connection to financing the client’s education, accommodation and living expenses abroad. All communication regarding client’s application is sent to the primary email address indicated in the consultation/application form. The client is expected to continue checking this email until the application letter is provided to the client for any updates to their application.
